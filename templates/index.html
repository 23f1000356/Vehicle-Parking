About ParkSmart

ParkSmart is a next-gen smart parking platform built to solve urban congestion and parking chaos using technology and automation.

Designed for both users and admins, it enables real-time booking, availability tracking, and seamless parking management. Admins can manage parking lots, monitor occupancy, and generate reports — all from a unified dashboard.

Whether you're a daily commuter or a parking operator, ParkSmart transforms your parking experience with AI-powered insights and intuitive design.
